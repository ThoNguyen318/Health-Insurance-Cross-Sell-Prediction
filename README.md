# 🚗 Health Insurance Cross Sell Prediction

## 📌 Objective
Predict customer propensity to purchase motor insurance from an existing health insurance customer base to optimize cross-selling efficiency. 

---

## 💼 Impact
Achieved an ROC-AUC of 0.84 and 0.86 Recall, ensuring maximum coverage without missing high-potential customers. 

---

## 📊 Key Insights
Utilized Cumulative Gain & Lift charts to divide customers into 3 distinct segments based on probability scores. 

---

## 🛠️ Solution
Recommended a Telesales approach for Segment A (capturing 54.2% of total buyers with only 20% effort), SMS/Email Automation for Segment B, and halted active outreach for Segment C to minimize operational waste.

---

## 📂 Repository Structure
- `Health Insurance Cross Sell Prediction.csv`: Raw dataset used for analysis.
- `Health_Insurance_Cross_Sell_Prediction.ipynb`: Complete Jupyter Notebook containing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), data preprocessing, Random Forest model implementation, and key business insights derived from the data.
- `README.md`: Project documentation.
